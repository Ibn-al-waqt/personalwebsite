Movies

Terminator

In the future, SKYNET has taken over the earth and a desperate resistance consisting of humanity's last survivors is led by John Connor. Skynet sends its assassin, the Terminator, to kill his mother while the resistance sends Kyle Reese to stop him.




Godzilla: King of the Monsters

On this earth, there are many monsters known as Titans and after the events of Godzilla, an eco-terrorist group is trying to awaken an ancient beast sealed in ice. King Ghidorah is unleased and commands the Titans to unleash destruction. Godzilla & Mothra team up to fight Ghidorah and Rodan for the sake of the world.



John Wick

John Wick is a retired assassin who lives with his dog after his wife passed away. One day, the son of a mafia boss comes in, beats up John, kills his dog, and steals his car. His father berates him for doing so and John starts hunting him down. Will John be able to take on the boss and receive his venegance?



Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle

Alex Vreeke picks up Jumanji, a board game, and takes it home.Later that night, Jumanji transforms into a video game and Alex disappears. 21 years later, 4 high school students pick up the game. They get sucked into the world of Jumanji, where beasts roam the wild lands & corrupt archaeologist Van Pelt has taken over. Can they beat the Van Pelt, restore the land and most importantly, go home?



Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Lost Ark

Indiana Jones is an archaeologist who goes around searching for ancient treasures and he gets wind of a Nazi operation to find the lost Ark of the Covenant. After reuniting with Marion, the daughter of his mentor, they set off to find clues of its whereabouts. Will the Ark fall into the hands of the Nazis, who want world domination?
