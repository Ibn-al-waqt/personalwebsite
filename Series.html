Series

Breaking Bad

Walter White, a chemistry teacher who has cancer, is looking for ways to secure his family's funding and future and he teams up with Jesse Pinkman to create crystal meth. They start gaining traction as an influential drug boss and soon, they have to watch out for rival bosses and the police.




The Flash

Barry Allen is a forensic assistant to the police and during his childhood, his mother was murdered and his father was framed for the crime, going to jail. One day in the lab, a bolt of lightining strikes him and when he wakes up from his coma, he gained a connection to the Speed Force, which gives him super speed. The Flash is now the fastest man alive and he is the hero of Central City.



Squid Game

Seong Gi-hun is an addicted gambler who lives with his mother and has financial difficulties. Out of desperation, he decides to join in a competition where they play childrens' games and win $45.6 billion won($38.6 million dollars). As the games go on, they cooperate and compete with each other to be the victor. The stakes of this life-and-death tournament gets higher and higher and the players will have to survive.



The Men Who Built America

This is a documentary and historical fiction film about the business titans of America during the Industrial Age and how Vanderbilt, Morgan, Carnegie, Rockefeller, and Ford made their legacies. Tensions, backstabbings, monopolies, and panics emerge and the true builders of America emerge.





Loki

During the events of Endgame, Loki escapes from the clutches of the Avengers and is taken to the Time Variance Authority(TVA). By escaping, he has disrupted the timeline and the future. Agent Mobius collaborates with Loki to take down a criminal, who turns out to be another version of Loki. Both Lokis team up to uncover the true mastermind behind the TVA and stop them from destroying thousands of worlds.
