Games

Sniper Elite 4

The Nazis have taken over Europe and this time, Karl Fairburne is sent to Italy to investigate and sabotage the Nazis to order to ensure the success of Operation Avalanche. During the game, you will be heading into islands, valleys, villages, monasteries, forrtresses and mansions to kill, sabotage, or steal. This game has some enjoyable gameplay and is a great game for snipers.




Star Wars Battlefront II

Immerse yourself in an intergalactic war, following Iden Verso, a member of the Empire's special forces. Players are fighting for both sides and many characters and abilities are available. Command fleets, droids, starships, Jedi knights, and Sith Lords. This is a great game for those wishing to experience the Star Wars universe and to engage in the various battles across the galaxy.



Super Mario Bros

This is the single most recognizable game in the world and everyone knows who says, "It's a me, Mario". Released in the 1980's, Mario must go on a journey to save Princess Peach from Bowser, the fearsome leader of the Koopas. Hit the bricks, gain powerups, jump on goombas, and watch out for the animals. This nostalgic game is old, but it is still fun to play.



Counter Strike 1.6

This is a great shooter game from the 1990's and early 2000's. With multiple maps, a variety of weapons, and engaging combat, this game is very enjoyable and iconic. Crouch, shoot, snipe, ambush, run, and do all you can to kill the other team and/or save hostages. A nostalgic but very fun and entertaining game.





Zuma

You have approached an ancient Inca temple, where you, as the frog, must stop a line of balls from falling into the hole. As you keep playing through the levels with ancient drawings on them, the line gets faster and longer but with powersups, you can advance to the top tiers of the temple and win. This is Grandpa's special game.
