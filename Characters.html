Characters

Jack Sparrow

As a pirate captain, Captain Jack Sparrow is an iconic character from the Pirates of the Caribbean series. Potrayed by Johhny Depp as an interesting, hilarious, yet sometimes crazy pirate who relies on his wits to outsmart his enemies. His charisma is immense, making everyone pay attention to him. Luck is on his side and as a master adventurer, he wins the treasure in the end, as always.




Indiana Jones

Indiana Jones is an archaeologist who travels the world, picking up treasures and avoiding violent tribes, the Nazis, theives, and most importantly, snakes. He has a good sense of optimism as everything somehow works out in the end. He isn't afraid to fight and save the day using his wits, logic, and strength. Indy cares for his father and family and witnesses the miracle of treasures together.



Tony Stark

Anthony Stark is the son of Howard Stark, an inventor and businessman, and as he grew up, he becomes a genius in engineering and he inherits Starks Industries, which manufactures weapons. As he escapes from terrorists in Afghanistan, he develops the Iron Man armored suits in order to restore peace and undo the destruction of civilians from his weapons.He has a good heart and works for the benefit of humanity.



John McClaine

John McClaine is the hero of Die Hard, where terrorists hijack a rich man's skyscraper in order to steal their money and John, as a police officer, is meeting a friend there. After the terrorists take over, John takes them on, defeats several of them, and rescues the hostages. John McClaine has immense wit, stamina, and intelligence and is one of the most iconic characters of the 1980's.





Rambo

John Rambo is a veteran from the Vietnam war and often, he faces injustice and fight back against it, whether it is in America, Vietnam, Afghanistan and more. He is a master at stealth, infiltration and assassination. Rambo stands up to protect those he cares about and is one of the best soldiers in the movies.
